What is AutoDock?

AutoDock is the future of hands-free charging — made for kids’ ride-on cars, golf carts, snowblowers, and more. Whether at home or at a commercial site, our innovative docking system automatically charges your vehicle when it's parked. It's as simple as drive, dock, and go.

  • ✅ Works with most Power Wheels and electric vehicles
  • ✅ Makes a perfect bundle gift for the holidays
  • ✅ Designed for garages, golf courses, and tool sheds
  • ✅ Built by real engineers — and parents — who care about safety

How It Works

AutoDock uses conductive aluminum plates and smart relays to automatically connect power to your ride-on vehicle. When your car docks into place, the plates align and power is delivered safely to recharge the battery—without needing to plug anything in.

FAQ

  • Is it safe for kids? Absolutely. AutoDock is designed with multiple safety layers, using low-voltage DC connections and isolated control logic.
  • What voltages does it support? 6V, 12V, 24V, and 48V systems with proper regulation.
  • Does it work with all ride-on cars? AutoDock is compatible with most models, including John Deere, Jeep, and custom builds.
