How it Works

From unbox to first charge in three simple steps.

Step 1

Place the Dock

Set the dock on a flat surface near a standard outlet. Plug in the included power unit.

Step 2

Attach Contact Kit

For most cars, use the included contact bracket under the vehicle — no wiring changes required.

Step 3

Park to Charge

LEDs confirm alignment and charging begins automatically. The dock stops when the battery is full.

FAQ

Everything you want to know about AutoDock.

Will AutoDock work with my child's car?

Most ride‑on cars are compatible using our universal contact bracket. We'll confirm fit during booking.

Is it safe?

Yes. The dock uses low voltage, current limiting, and polarity protection. Contacts are recessed and only live when a vehicle is docked.

Do you install it?

We ship anywhere. Simple DIY install; we can assist via video if you like. Typical install is under 30 minutes.

What about weather?

Home Dock is for covered spaces; Home Dock+ adds outdoor‑ready hardware. Pro systems can be fully weather‑sealed.

Can it charge other things?

Yes — we have adapters for scooters, small carts, and outdoor toys. Ask about Pro.
