Frequently Asked Questions

Is AutoDock safe for children?

Yes. AutoDock is low-voltage and protected by smart design to prevent accidental contact or misuse.

What vehicles work with it?

Most Power Wheels, electric ride-ons, golf carts, and small yard vehicles can be adapted.

Do I need to modify my car?

Some light wiring to connect to the charging plates may be required. We provide instructions and kits.
